HONEST READING: on the PyTorch backend the inner fit is 96.8% of per-candidate co-search time,
so it is THE bottleneck; NDVM cuts it 8000x+ and removes it. The robust headline is calibration
throughput (above). End-to-end, removing a 96.8% bottleneck still collapses per-candidate time
from minutes to (no-LLM) milliseconds or (live-LLM) the LLM floor -- so NDVM exposes the LLM as
the next bottleneck rather than claiming an unbounded end-to-end discovery speedup.
